GAME: Minecraft
OBJECTIVE: Survive, Build, Explore.

KEY ENTITIES:
- Creeper: Green, explodes. Run away or hit-and-run.
- Zombie: Green skin, burns in sun. Easy target.
- Skeleton: Shoots arrows. Hide or strafe.
- Diamond: Light blue ore. MINE IT IMMEDIATELY.
- Wood: Brown logs. Break to craft.

STRATEGY:
- Night is dangerous. Build shelter or dig hole.
- Food is essential. Kill pigs/cows or eat bread.
- If low health, eat.
- If see deep cave, place torches.

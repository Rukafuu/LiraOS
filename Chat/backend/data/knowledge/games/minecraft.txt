GAME: MINECRAFT SURVIVAL
High Frequency Loop (<1s decisions).

CONTROLS:
- W,A,S,D: Move
- SPACE: Jump (Hold to swim up)
- SHIFT: Sneak (Dont fall off edges)
- LEFT_CLICK (Hold): Mine Block / Attack
- RIGHT_CLICK: Place Block / Use Item / Open Door
- E: Inventory
- ESC: Pause
- 1-9: Select Hotbar Slot

SURVIVAL STRATEGY:
1. PUNCH TREES: Find a tree, aim at trunk, HOLD LEFT CLICK until it breaks. Collect LOGS.
2. CRAFT PLANKS: Open Inventory (E), put LOGS in crafting area -> Get PLANKS.
3. CRAFTING TABLE: 4 Planks (2x2) -> Crafting Table.
4. TOOLS:
   - Sticks: 2 Planks vertical.
   - Pickaxe: 3 Planks (top row) + 2 Sticks (middle col) in Table.
5. STONE AGE: Use Wooden Pickaxe to mine STONE (Grey). Don't use hand for stone!
6. SHELTER: Before night falls (monsters spawn), dig a hole in a mountain or build a hut with dirt/planks.
7. FOOD: Punch cows/pigs/chickens. Cook meat in Furnace (8 Cobblestone in circle).

COMBAT:
- Aim at enemy.
- CLICK FAST (1.9+ cooldown: Wait for sword icon to recharge for max damage).
- Critical Hit: Jump and hit while falling.

VISION TIPS:
- Green Exploding Thing: CREEPER. RUN AWAY. Do not fight without bow usually.
- Green/Rotten Flesh: ZOMBIE. Easy to kill.
- Skeleton: Strafe left/right to dodge arrows.
- Enderman (Tall/Black): DO NOT LOOK IN EYES.

NAVIGATION:
- Sun rises East, sets West.
- Clouds move West.
- If lost, dig up to high ground to look around.
- Don't dig straight down (Lava hazard).

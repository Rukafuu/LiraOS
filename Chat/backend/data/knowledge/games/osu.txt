GAME: osu!
OBJECTIVE: Click circles to the beat.

KEY ENTITIES:
- HitCircle: Numbered circle with approach ring. Click when ring hits edge.
- Slider: Ball on track. Hold and follow.
- Spinner: Spin mouse in circles fast.

STRATEGY:
- Rhythm is key. Listen to music.
- Don't panic on streams (fast circles).
- Acc (Accuracy) matters.
- If you miss, say "Sb" or "Lag".
